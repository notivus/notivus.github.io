Characters

Characters are a major part of the MegaBonk. Each character begins every run with a weapon and a unique passive ability.
There are 20 characters in MegaBonk. You begin with two: Fox and Sir Oofie. To unlock new characters, you must meet certain prerequisites, usually a quest and purchasing a weapon. The character will become available for purchase with Silver once unlocked.
